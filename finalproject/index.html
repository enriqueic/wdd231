Shrinkflation Watch project document

Purpose

Shrinkflation Watch helps you track, visualize, inform yourself, and report changes in product sizes and prices.

  • Browse Products: See real-world examples of shrinkflation. Save your items and make your own personalized watch-list!
  • Visualize Trends: Explore statistics and charts about product changes over time.
  • Share Your Experience: Report shrinkflation or leave comments for the community.
Browse Products See Statistics Report/Comment

Scenarios

  • I have learned about shrinkflation recently. Where can I find relevant news or examples of it?
  • How can I compare the price and size changes of my favorite products over time?
  • How can I check if a product I buy has been affected by shrinkflation?
  • How do I report a new case of shrinkflation I have noticed?
