Shrinkflation Watch

Purpose

Shrinkflation Watch helps you track, visualize, inform yourself, and report changes in product sizes and prices.

  • Browse Products: See real-world examples of shrinkflation. Save your items and make your own personalized watch-list!
  • Visualize Trends: Explore statistics and charts about product changes over time.

  • How do I report a new case of shrinkflation I have noticed?
